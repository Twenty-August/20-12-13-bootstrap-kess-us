About US

Hi
We are KESS LLC, a small startup based out of beautiful Wisconsin. All we do is make board games. And Kess is our first product. Kess is a game of strategy that is really fast moving, it is suitable for those of us who like games to move fast and we get to win or lose fast. And it is 100% skill based, there is no element of luck, so if you have a fast brain, you could be really good at Kess. It really has been a long long time since a really original board game came out. So we thought, let us make one that is original, moves fast, allows strategic thinking , and yet is super fun for the whole family. We are working on the online version of Kess, but nothing beats being able to play with a real board and real pieces. Kess is the kind of game you could play on your porch or on the beach or at the park. Everybody gets to have a whole lot of fun, and that is what matters.
Kess On!
Dave
KESS LLC, Wisconsin
